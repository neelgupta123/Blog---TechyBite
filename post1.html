What is web hosting? Why is it used?

Web hosting definition. When a hosting provider allocates space on a web server for a website to store its files, they are hosting a website. Web hosting makes the files that comprise a website (code, images, etc.) available for viewing online. All the files that are being used to make a website are stored on a server location. When you upload the files, they are downloaded from there and store on a disk so that anyone on the internet can access the files. If you want a site to be hosted on the internet from your house, you should have a PC/Laptop that should be turned on 24/7 and needs a router for port forwarding.

Chances of a website going down

Suppose you buy good hosting services, then there 0.01% of going your site down. The site going down means there can be electricity or internet issues in the data centers. So, their servers can be turned off so your website can be down. To avoid this you should buy a premium hosting (purchased)

Full Control on the site

Blogging platforms like Medium, Blogger, Tumblr, LinkedIn, wordpress.com does not give full access to the site, so you can't make many changes. But if you make a site from wordpress.org or install WordPress from your hosting provider, you have full access to your site and make any changes.

Storage

Storage is a widespread issue among people using free hosting because most of them only give 2GB or max. 10GB storage is not enough for any site made on WordPress or a heavy HTML & CSS site. And if you want to make an e-commerce site, your min. Need 10GB storage for big sites.

Ranking

Let's see how free hosting affects Ranking. When free hosting gives you a free domain name like mysite.subdomain.com, this is a subdomain. The Main domain has bought by the free hosting company. Any subdomain does not rank easily as the Main domain does. They also give ads on the sites. And the loading speed of the site is shallow, so and search engine don't want that their users suffer from the slow speed of any site

Bandwidth

Bandwidth is the internet speed and the amount of internet transferred from the servers to the client(user). If you want your website to be fast, then you should have good bandwidth and Do you know that how much GB bandwidth you have depends on the no. of users who can visit your site in a month?

Customer support should be 24/7

Customer support in most of the paid hosting services gives 24/7 support. But in free hosting, most of them didn't even reply. In some, they reply in 2-4 weeks. Customer support is essential because if the user is facing problems, issues, or need help in the site's control panel. It can; How to update PHP to the new version or; How to install WordPress on the website.

Security

Security of your website is very important as if your website is not secure, your users will not trust you.For security you should make a private policy page and get a SSL Certificate.

What does SSL do?

SSL is a certificate that ensures users that the site is safe and secure. But you can't get an SSL certificate for free. It makes a lock sign on the URL's left side, and you have to buy it. You can buy it from Godaddy, Hostinger, Namecheap, etc.
